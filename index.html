Welcome! I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computational Pathology at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen, and currently a joint training student at Leibniz-Institut für Analytische Wissenschaften – ISAS – e.V. My research focuses on nuclei segmentation, classification, and the application of multimodal AI in cancer diagnosis.
